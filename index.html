Simon Game Rules
The Simon Game is a memory-based challenge where players must repeat an ever-increasing sequence of lights and sounds. The rules are as follows:

1. Starting the Game: The game begins with a simple pattern consisting of a sequence of colored lights and sounds.
2. Sequence Playback: A color is randomly chosen, and a sound is played corresponding to that color.
3. Player's Turn: The player must repeat the sequence by pressing the colored buttons in the exact order presented.
4. Increasing Difficulty: With each successful repetition, the game adds one more color to the sequence, making the pattern longer and more complex.
5. Game Over: If the player fails to repeat the sequence correctly, the game ends, and the player is shown their final score, typically reflecting the longest sequence they successfully completed.
6. Objective: The goal is to memorize and correctly repeat the longest possible sequence of colors without making any mistakes.
